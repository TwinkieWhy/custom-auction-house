How to Publish
1
Add all your items using the form above. They appear in the list on the right.
2
Click Copy JSON or Download to get your items.json file.
3
In your GitHub repo, open items.json and paste the contents (or upload the downloaded file).
4
Upload item images into the images/ folder in your repo. Reference them as images/filename.png.
5
Commit and push. Your GitHub Pages site will update within a minute.
